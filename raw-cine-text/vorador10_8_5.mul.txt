-- Avernus is in flames...
and with it,
our hopes may evaporate.
-- How can I find the Heart in the midst of such chaos?
-- You must act quickly.
But beware - an ancient evil dwells within,
long
Unspoken among our kind.
Undoubtedly this is the source of the corruption
that infects the Circle.
If you are to succeed,
you must resist its influence.
